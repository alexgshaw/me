Fitness

Ever since my senior year of high school, I have been passionate about physical fitness. I feel like physical fitness is neglected by most people, despite being one of the most direct influences on our mental and physical wellbeing. If you start working out regularly, you begin feeling better surprisingly fast. One of the most interesting lessons I have learned at BYU was that your brain only produces new neurons during sleep or during exercise. So maybe some people should put down their homework for a second and go workout. It could end up saving you time!
